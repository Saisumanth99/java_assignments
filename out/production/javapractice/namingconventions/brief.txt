StringBuilder class :

    - The classname is in upper camel case.
    - The method names are in lower camel case except the constructors. Ex. append(), capacity(), ensureCapacity()
    - methods are public.
    - methods are verbs.
    - variables are also in lower camel case, ex. offset, endIndex, startIndex.



LocalDateTime class :

    - The classname is in upper camel case.
    - The method names are in lower camel case except the constructors. Ex. get(), format(), minusDays()
    - methods are public.
    - methods are verbs.
    - Constants are in capital letters and follows snake case.
    - variables are also in lower camel case, ex. days, time, formatter.
